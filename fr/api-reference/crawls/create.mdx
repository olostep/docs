---
title: 'Create Crawl'
description: 'Starts a new crawl. You receive a `id` to track the progress. The operation may take 1-10 mins depending upon the site and depth and pages parameters.'
openapi: POST /v1/crawls
---

<TIP>
**GET NOTIFIED ON COMPLETION:** PASS THE `WEBHOOK` PARAMETER WITH YOUR ENDPOINT URL TO RECEIVE AN HTTP POST WHEN THE CRAWL COMPLETES. SEE [WEBHOOKS](/API-REFERENCE/COMMON/WEBHOOKS) FOR DETAILS.
</TIP>
