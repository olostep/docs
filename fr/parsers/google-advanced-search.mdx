**ID:** @OLOSTEP/GOOGLE-ADVANCED-SEARCH

**DESCRIPTION:**
THE GOOGLE ADVANCED SEARCH PARSER EXTRACTS COMPREHENSIVE DATA FROM GOOGLE SEARCH RESULTS, INCLUDING ADVANCED FILTERS AND METADATA. IT IS IDEAL FOR IN-DEPTH RESEARCH AND DATA ANALYSIS.

**USE CASES:**
- PERFORM DETAILED SEARCH ANALYSIS
- GATHER FILTERED SEARCH RESULTS FOR RESEARCH
- AUTOMATE ADVANCED DATA COLLECTION
