**DESCRIPTION:**
THE LINKEDIN PROFILE PARSER ALLOWS YOU TO EXTRACT DETAILED AND STRUCTURED INFORMATION FROM PUBLIC LINKEDIN USER PROFILES. THIS INCLUDES WORK EXPERIENCE, EDUCATION, SKILLS, AND OTHER PROFESSIONAL DATA, MAKING IT EASIER TO AUTOMATE DATA COLLECTION FOR RESEARCH, RECRUITING, OR ANALYTICS.

**USE CASES:**
- BUILD TALENT DATABASES FOR RECRUITMENT
- ANALYZE PROFESSIONAL BACKGROUNDS FOR BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE
- ENRICH CRM SYSTEMS WITH UP-TO-DATE PROFILE DATA
