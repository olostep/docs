**DESCRIPTION:**
THE GOOGLE MAPS PARSER EXTRACTS LOCATION AND BUSINESS DATA FROM GOOGLE MAPS, INCLUDING ADDRESSES, RATINGS, AND REVIEWS. IT IS USEFUL FOR BUILDING LOCATION-BASED APPLICATIONS AND BUSINESS DIRECTORIES.

**USE CASES:**
- GATHER BUSINESS DATA FOR MAPPING APPS
- COLLECT REVIEWS AND RATINGS FOR ANALYSIS
- BUILD LOCAL BUSINESS DIRECTORIES
