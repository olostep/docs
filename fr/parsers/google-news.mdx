**ID:** @OLOSTEP/GOOGLE-NEWS  
**CREDITS:** 1

**DESCRIPTION:**
THE GOOGLE NEWS PARSER EXTRACTS NEWS ARTICLES AND METADATA FROM GOOGLE NEWS, MAKING IT EASY TO MONITOR CURRENT EVENTS AND TRENDS. IT IS IDEAL FOR MEDIA MONITORING AND NEWS AGGREGATION.

**USE CASES:**
- AGGREGATE NEWS ARTICLES FOR MEDIA MONITORING
- TRACK BREAKING NEWS AND TRENDS
- AUTOMATE NEWS CURATION
