**Description:**
The Gemini Search parser extracts search results from Gemini (beta), providing access to experimental and advanced search capabilities. It is ideal for users who want to explore new search technologies and gather unique insights.

**Use Cases:**
- Access experimental search results for research
- Compare Gemini results with other search engines
- Test and evaluate new search features
