**Description:**
The LinkedIn Company parser extracts comprehensive company information from LinkedIn, such as company size, industry, location, and employee insights. This helps automate market research and competitor analysis.

**Use Cases:**
- Research competitors and market trends
- Enrich B2B lead lists with company data
- Monitor company growth and changes
