**Description:**
The Google Shopping parser extracts product listings, prices, and details from Google Shopping. It is useful for price comparison, product research, and e-commerce analytics.

**Use Cases:**
- Compare product prices across retailers
- Monitor product availability and trends
- Automate e-commerce data collection
