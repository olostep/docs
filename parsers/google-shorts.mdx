**Description:**
The Google Shorts parser extracts data from Google Shorts, including video metadata, views, and engagement. It is ideal for analyzing short-form video trends and content performance.

**Use Cases:**
- Analyze short video content for trends
- Track engagement and performance of Shorts
- Collect data for social media analytics
