**Description:**
The Brave Search parser extracts data from Brave Search results, providing privacy-focused search data for research and analysis. It is ideal for users who value privacy and want alternative search perspectives.

**Use Cases:**
- Retrieve search results for privacy-focused research
- Compare Brave results with other search engines
- Monitor search trends in privacy-centric environments
