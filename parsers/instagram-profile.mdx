**Description:**
The Instagram Profile parser extracts structured data from public Instagram profiles, including follower counts, bio, posts, and engagement metrics. This is useful for influencer marketing and social media analytics.

**Use Cases:**
- Identify and analyze influencers
- Track competitor social media presence
- Collect data for marketing campaigns
